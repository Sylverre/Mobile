Asset Details Screen

From the Alerts, select a single alert to view Alert details. SIelect the Alert Source (in this example, Rack Temp 1-2-2-1) to drill down to that asset and its asset details.

Now if you select the Alert Source, you will be taken to the Asset Details for the asset involved in this alert.
