Asset Manager Mobile Specifications & Minimum Requirements

 

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

Asset Manager Mobile must be used with Asset Manager version 1.0 or higher.

 

OPERATING SYSTEM SUPPORT

ØiOS Device Support:

  • iOS version: 5.1 or higher
  • iPad support: iPad 1 and higher
  • iPhone support: iPhone 4 and higher

 

ØAndroid Device Support:

  • Android version: 4.0 and higher
  • Device Requirements: 640x480 screen size or higher
