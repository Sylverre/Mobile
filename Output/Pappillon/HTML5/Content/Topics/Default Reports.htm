Pre-Built Reports

  • Current Inventory Report: Each Individual Location
  • Asset Movement Report: All Locations (Similar to Apple Movement Report)
  • Door Access Report: Shows Min/Max/Ave time each door was open, lists events where doors were open longer than their specified max duration, and shows all events when critical doors were opened
  • Environmental Thresholds Report: For a single location, shows detailed trends via charts, lists events when thresholds were exceeded, Min/Max/Ave values, etc.
  • Operational Status Report: shows all assets in use, in storage, etc.
  • Storage Report: All Storage Locations (Similar to Apple Storage Report.) Shows assets that have been in storage 30, 60, 90, and 120+ days. Does not show decommissioning/decommissioned assets)
  • Storage Lifetime Exceeded Report: Lists all assets whose total days in storage exceeds the configured max. Does not show decommissioning/decommissioned assets
  • Assets Not In Service Report: Shows the duration assets are spending in storage after they are taken out of service
  • Ownership Report: Shows asset counts and trends by owner for each specified location and lists ownership of individual assets
  • Planned Lifecycle Report: Charts aggregating assets based on their current age compared to their planned replacement date. Also shows remaining time until planned replacement of each individual asset
  • Detected Location Report: Shows actual location of tagged assets
  • Location Audit Report: Compares actual location to specified location
  • Product Inventory Report: An inventory report showing aggregated totals for each make & model and also listing all assets grouped by their make & model.
  • Containment Report: Shows current values and trends for U-space utilization, environmentals, door status, and asset counts and lists each asset in each containment
  • CRAC Status Report: Shows CRAC Unit supply, return, and RTI values. Also charts showing trends over time
  • Data center environmental report: Similar to current rack environmental reports that we use on datacenter.rfcode.com and which many customers use
  • Power Monitoring report(s)
  • U-Space Capacity Report: Shows U-space capacity and U-space availability
